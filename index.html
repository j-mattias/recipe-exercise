Classic Lasagna

  • Time: ~60min
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Portions: 4

Who doesn't like lasagna? With this recipe you lasagna will be perfect with a soft and nice consitency, with great taste!

Ingredients

  • Yellow Onion: x2
  • Garlic Cloves: x2
  • Minced Meat: 500 g
  • Oil: 1 msk
  • Tomato Purée: 4 msk
  • Dried Thyme: 1 tsk
  • Dried Rosemary: 1 tsk
  • Crushed Tomatoes: 390 g
  • Meat Bouillon Cube: x1
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lasagna Sheets: x9

Sauce

  • Butter: 6 msk (~90 g)
  • Flour: 6 msk
  • Milk: 10 dl
  • Grated parmesan: 2 dl

Instructions

  1. Heat the oven to 175°C
  2. Peel and chop onions and garlic. Sauté the minced meat, onion, and garlic in the oil in a frying pan. Add tomato paste and let it sauté with the meat. Season with thyme and rosemary. Add crushed tomatoes and a bouillon cube, and let it simmer for about 10 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Sauce: Melt the butter and stir in the flour. Gradually add the milk while whisking. Let the sauce simmer for about 5 minutes. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Layer sauce, lasagna sheets, and the minced meat sauce in an ovenproof dish (approximately 20 x 30 cm). Finish with sauce and cheese on top.
  5. Place the lasagna in the center of the oven for ~40 minutes.
